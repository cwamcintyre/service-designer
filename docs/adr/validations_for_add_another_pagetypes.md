# Architectural Decision Record (ADR)

## How validation works for "Add Another"

## Context

Provide background information and context for the decision. Include:

- The problem being solved.
- Relevant constraints or requirements.
- Any assumptions made.

## Decision

State the decision clearly. Include:

- What was decided.
- Why this decision was made.

## Consequences

Describe the consequences of the decision. Include:

- Positive outcomes.
- Negative outcomes or trade-offs.
- Any follow-up actions required.

## Alternatives Considered

List the alternatives that were considered and why they were not chosen. Include:

- Pros and cons of each alternative.
- Reasons for rejecting them.

## References

Provide links or references to supporting documentation, research, or discussions.

- Accepted
